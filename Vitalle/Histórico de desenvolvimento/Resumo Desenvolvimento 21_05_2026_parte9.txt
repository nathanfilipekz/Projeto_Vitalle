    · public: true
    · file_size_limit: 52428800 (50 MB)

      quicktime, avi, msword, docx

  Storage policies:
    · patient_attachments_select: SELECT TO anon, authenticated
    · patient_attachments_insert: INSERT TO anon, authenticated
    · patient_attachments_delete: DELETE TO anon, authenticated

  Tabela: public.patient_attachments
    id           uuid PK
    tenant_id    uuid FK → tenants
    patient_id   uuid FK → patients
    file_name    text
    file_type    text (MIME)
    file_size    bigint (bytes)
    storage_path text (caminho no bucket)
    public_url   text (URL pública do Storage)
    created_at   timestamptz
    deleted_at   timestamptz (soft delete)

  RLS: attachments_mvp_anon (FOR ALL TO anon, authenticated)

3.2 Serviço: attachments-service.ts  [CRIADO]
  Helpers:
    formatFileSize(bytes) → "1.2 MB"
    fileCategory(mimeType) → 'image' | 'pdf' | 'video' | 'doc' | 'other'

  Funções:
    listAttachments(tenantId, patientId) → AttachmentRow[]
    uploadAttachment(tenantId, patientId, file):

    deleteAttachment(attachment, tenantId):
      1. Remove arquivo do Storage
